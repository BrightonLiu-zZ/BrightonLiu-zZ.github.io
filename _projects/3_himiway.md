### Overview

At Himiway Intelligent Technology USA, I designed the data infrastructure and predictive models supporting strategic retail expansion decisions for an e-bike company operating across multiple US regions.

### ETL Architecture

- **Architected a scalable SQL/Python ETL pipeline** for e-bike sales data ingestion and forecasting.
- Refactored experimental Jupyter notebooks into a **production-ready modular codebase** using `argparse`, structured logging, and `Makefile` orchestration for continuous data ingestion.
- Implemented automated data quality checks at each pipeline stage.

### Forecasting Model

- Developed and deployed an **end-to-end XGBoost model** to forecast next-quarter demand by city and ZIP code.
- Used time-series cross-validation to prevent data leakage in temporally ordered data.
- Model outputs directly informed regional store location selection and inventory allocation.
- **Result: accelerated the executive decision-making cycle by 75%** (from 4 weeks to 1 week).

### A/B Test Design & Analysis

- Designed and evaluated a **months-long strategic A/B test** on in-store customer experience: fat-tire vs. thin-tire bike display configuration.
- Built **Tableau** dashboards tracking Conversion Rate and Revenue per Visitor as primary KPIs.
- Findings drove data-informed changes to retail floor layout and regional inventory mix.
